Student Feedback Highlights

Many students reported issues with cleanliness around water coolers and dissatisfaction with water quality, indicating areas for improvement in infrastructure and maintenance.

Remarks

  • TDS levels exceed recommended limits in all tested departments, indicating poor water quality.
  • pH levels are within acceptable ranges, indicating the water is safe for drinking.
  • The non-functional cooler in the Workshop department hampers accessibility.
  • Turbidity levels indicate clear water with no suspended particles.

Recommendations

  1. Install water purification systems to reduce TDS levels and improve water quality.
  2. Repair and maintain non-functional coolers, especially in the Workshop department.
  3. Ensure regular cleaning of water tanks and coolers to maintain hygiene.
  4. Conduct routine monitoring of water quality to address potential issues promptly.
